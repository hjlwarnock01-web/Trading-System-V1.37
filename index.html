1. Entry Rules
Entry Type Conditions
Long Entry 1
(Type 1)
  • EMA 3H > EMA 1D
  • 1H Demand Zone
  • 15m Liquidity Grab from low
  • Price < EMA 3H
  • 15m Internal Demand Zone
Short Entry 1
(Type 1)
  • EMA 3H < EMA 1D
  • 1H Supply Zone
  • 15m Liquidity Grab from high
  • Price > EMA 3H
  • 15m Internal Supply Zone
Long Entry 2
(Type 2)
  • EMA 3H < EMA 1D
  • M Zone hit
  • 1H Demand Zone or CHoCH Zone
  • 15m Liquidity Grab from low
  • 15m Demand Zone
  • Price < EMA 3H
Short Entry 2
(Type 2)
  • EMA 3H > EMA 1D
  • M Zone hit
  • 1H Supply Zone or CHoCH Zone
  • 15m Liquidity Grab from high
  • 15m Supply Zone
  • Price > EMA 3H

Entry Type Confirmation Methods:

  • Red 15m Candle
  • 2m CHoCH Pattern
  • 2m First Pullback after CHoCH

6. Glossary
EMA (Exponential Moving Average)
Weighted moving average giving more importance to recent price data.
M Zones
Higher timeframe (1D, 12H, 3D) supply or demand zones.
1H Zones
Key supply or demand levels formed on the 1-hour chart.
15m Zones
Internal supply or demand levels on the 15-minute chart.
LQG (Liquidity Grab)
A price move that sweeps stop losses/liquidity before reversing.
CHoCH (Change of Character)
A shift in market structure that signals a potential reversal.
Supply Zone
Area where selling pressure is expected to emerge.
Demand Zone
Area where buying pressure is expected to emerge.
Drawdown
Reduction in account equity from peak to trough.
SL (Stop Loss)
Predefined level where a trade is closed to prevent further loss.
